# De-identification Standard

## Hard Rules
- No names, addresses, phone numbers, emails, or direct identifiers.
- No exact full birth dates in public artifacts.
- No record-level free text that may contain identifiers.

## Pseudonymization
- Use irreversible patient pseudonyms.
- Keep linkage keys in a separate secured system outside this repository.

## Publication Controls
- Aggregate reporting only for public outputs.
- Suppress cells with very small counts where re-identification risk is elevated.

## Audit
- Every export must log date, actor, purpose, and approval reference.
