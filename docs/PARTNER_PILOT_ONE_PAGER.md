# Partner Pilot One-Pager

## Product
SSV OncoSurgery Evidence Platform

AI-assisted clinical outcomes intelligence for surgical oncology programs.

## Target Partners
- Oncology and surgical hospitals
- Academic clinical centers
- Research collaboratives and multicenter networks

## Pilot Scope (12 Weeks)
1. Data onboarding
- De-identified retrospective cohort ingestion (30-200 cases).
- Data quality and schema harmonization.

2. Evidence pipeline
- Automated quarterly report generation.
- KPI dashboard for LOS, complications, readmission, mortality.
- Protocol drift and pathway adherence review.

3. Enablement
- Clinical + data steward training sessions.
- Governance and publication workflow setup.

## Deliverables
- Baseline quality report.
- Quarterly evidence report.
- Risk-adjusted analysis snapshot.
- Governance checklist and interoperability mapping note.

## Pricing Model (Pilot)
- Starter Pilot: USD 9,000

- Pro Pilot: USD 18,000

- Support response:
  - Critical: < 4 business hours
