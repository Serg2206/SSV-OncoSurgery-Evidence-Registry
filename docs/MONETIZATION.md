# Monetization Strategy

## Positioning
Evidence-to-Impact platform for surgical oncology:
- protocol quality intelligence,
- outcomes analytics,
- publication acceleration,
- educational premium content.

## Revenue Streams
1. B2B Clinical Analytics Subscription
- Monthly subscription for hospital quality dashboards and quarterly evidence packs.

2. Research Collaboration Packages
- Paid multicenter analysis support and publication co-development.

3. Training and Certification
- Courses/workshops on evidence registry operations, AI-assisted analysis, and protocol governance.

4. Premium Data Products
- Curated de-identified benchmark datasets and model cards for approved partners.

5. Advisory and Implementation
- Setup services for registry architecture, governance, and deployment.

## Product Tiers
- Starter: local deployment, basic QA + quarterly reports.
- Pro: advanced risk adjustment, trend analysis, protocol drift detection.
- Enterprise: SSO, audit, multi-site support, private cloud/on-prem, custom SLAs.

## Guardrails
- Comply with local/international privacy requirements.
- Never commercialize identifiable data.
- Publish transparent limitations and bias statements.
