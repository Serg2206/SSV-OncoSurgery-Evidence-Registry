# SSV Evidence Pilot

Clinical AI + evidence operations for surgical oncology teams.

## What You Get in 12 Weeks
1. De-identified registry onboarding.
2. Automated data quality checks and weekly quality summaries.
3. Quarterly evidence report with reproducible outputs.
4. Partner-ready KPI dashboard endpoint.
5. Governance and interoperability starter pack (FHIR/OMOP mapping direction).

## Why It Matters
- Faster internal quality loops.
- Stronger publication pipeline.
- Scalable foundation for multi-site research.
- Direct path to productized clinical analytics services.
